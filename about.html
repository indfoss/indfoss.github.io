About Indfoss

Indfoss Refrigeration Incorporated has been serving the Bay Area for over 20 years. We specialize in excellent commercial refrigeration service for all types of businesses. We also service our residential customers.

The owner, Tejinder Singh Bharaj, has over 30 years of in-field experience. We are located in Union City, California. We are a licensed contractors and we offer 30 day labor warranty on each of our services.

Our clients range from fast food restaurants like Denny’s, Chevy’s Mexican Grill, Subway restaurants, and Bombay Garden to commercial businesses like Chevron gas stations, Arco Gas, liquor stores and many more restaurants, markets, and liquor stores.
